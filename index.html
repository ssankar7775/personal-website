About Me

Hello! I’m Tosh Sankar, a passionate Aerospace Engineer with experience in spacecraft systems design with a focus on Communications and Avionics (hardware and software), 3D printing, and mechanical systems (structural and fluids). I’m excited about working on advanced space and defense systems and making space more accessible through cutting-edge engineering.
My first exposure to Aerospace Engineering was an accidental sighting of the first successful landing of the Falcon 9 in 2015. Since then I have been inspired by the concept of sustainable and easier access to the space domain to build infrastructure and have easier acces for new scientific research.

During my time in my undergrad at The University of Michigan, I have engaged in various different project teams from learning about composites and mechanical design with MASA (Michigan Aeronautical Science Associati on) as well as Model Based Systems Engineering with the Michigan Vertical Flight technology team and the CubeSat Flight Lab.

Please use this website as a reference to better understand my experience on my portfolio page and feel free to download a copy of my resume below! If you have any questions or have any open positions, please contact me with the contact form or connect with me on LinkedIn!
